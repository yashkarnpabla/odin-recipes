Hot Chocolate Recipe

Indulge in the warmth and richness of homemade hot chocolate!

A comforting cup of hot chocolate is a winter favorite for all ages. Try this easy and delicious hot chocolate recipe for a cozy and indulgent treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup semisweet or bittersweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings for topping (optional)

Recipe Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat milk, heavy cream, and sugar over medium heat. Whisk occasionally until the mixture is hot but not boiling.
  2. Gradually whisk in cocoa powder until smooth and well combined.
  3. Add chopped chocolate to the mixture and continue whisking until the chocolate is completely melted.
  4. Stir in vanilla extract and a pinch of salt. Continue to heat until the hot chocolate reaches your desired temperature.
  5. Remove from heat and pour the hot chocolate into mugs.
  6. If desired, top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade hot chocolate!
