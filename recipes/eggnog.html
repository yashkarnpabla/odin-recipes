Eggnog Recipe

Enjoy the creamy and festive taste of homemade eggnog!

Whether sipped by the fireplace or spiked with your favorite spirits, eggnog is a classic holiday treat. Try this delicious homemade eggnog recipe for a rich and flavorful experience.

Ingredients:

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: 1/2 cup bourbon or rum for a spiked version
  • Ground nutmeg for garnish

Recipe Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until well combined and slightly thickened.
  2. In a saucepan, heat milk, cream, vanilla extract, nutmeg, cinnamon, and salt over medium heat. Bring to a simmer, but do not boil.
  3. Gradually whisk the hot milk mixture into the egg yolk mixture. Pour the combined mixture back into the saucepan.
  4. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the eggnog thickens and reaches a temperature of 160°F (71°C). Do not let it boil.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to chill.
  6. If desired, add bourbon or rum for a spiked version.
  7. Before serving, whisk the eggnog to ensure a smooth consistency. Pour into glasses and garnish with ground nutmeg.
  8. Enjoy your homemade eggnog!
